Scripture Baseball

Pick a standard work, a language, and a difficulty. Then guess the book and chapter from a verse.

Book guess = 5 points Chapter guess = 10 points Randomized answer choices Powered by your GitHub data files

How it works

1. Pick your set Choose a language, difficulty, and one standard work.
2. Read the verse The reference is hidden. You guess the book first.
3. Guess the chapter After the book guess, the correct book is shown. Then you guess the chapter.
4. Score points 5 points for the correct book and 10 points for the correct chapter.
Vietnamese supports Book of Mormon, Doctrine and Covenants, and Pearl of Great Price only.
